Plugins

Roulette

A plugin for drawing roulette curves like cycloids, epicycloids, and hypocycloids by rolling a circle with markers plotting the curves.

This allows for the simulation of a circle rolling along a straight line or another circle with markers tracing cycloidal curves, offering dynamic visuals to explore the geometry of roulette curves.

Fractal

A plugin for generating fractals and visualizing their intricate patterns at varying levels of complexity.
